# Coolest PFP of All Time

A social leaderboard for X profile pictures.

Sign in with your X account, have your profile picture automatically imported, and let the community decide where you rank. Users can rate other profiles, compete on the leaderboard, and battle for the title of the coolest PFP on the internet.

## Features

* Sign in with X
* Automatically fetch and display your X profile picture
* Community-driven rating system
* Global leaderboard and rankings
* Discover and rate other profiles

Built as a fun social experiment around online identity, internet culture, and competitive rankings.
